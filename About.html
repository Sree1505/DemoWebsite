History:

Initially, BMW focused on aircraft engines, then motorcycles, and finally automobiles. The company was formed from the merger of Rapp Motorenwerke and Gustav Otto Flugmaschinenfabrik. The BMW logo, featuring a blue and white checkered pattern, is a nod to the Bavarian flag and BMW's origins in Bavaria.

Key Models and Series:

  • BMW Series: A range of sedans, coupes, convertibles, and hatchbacks.


  • BMW X Series: SUVs, including models like the X1, X3, X5, and X7.

  • BMW i Series: All-electric models like the i3, i4, and iX.

  • BMW M: Performance-oriented models, often with enhanced engines and sporty styling.

  • BMW Z: Roadsters and sports cars.
