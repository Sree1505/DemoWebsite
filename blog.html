The all new BMW i7

The BMW i7 is a luxury all-electric sedan known for its opulent interior, cutting-edge technology, and impressive performance. It offers a spacious and comfortable cabin, especially in the rear, with features like a fold-down "Theatre Screen" and a unique "Interaction Bar". While it's a large and heavy car, it manages to handle well and provides a satisfying driving experience, particularly for long journeys.

Driving Experience:

While the i7 is a large and heavy car, it manages to deliver a comfortable and engaging driving experience. The electric motors provide instant torque, and the car handles well on both highways and twisty roads. The ride quality is excellent, and the car remains quiet even at high speeds.

BMW M4 CS Launched

The new BMW M4 CS (Coupe Sport) has been launched, slotting between the M4 Competition and the track-focused M4 CSL. It features a 3.0-liter inline-six engine producing 550 horsepower, paired with an 8-speed M Steptronic transmission and M xDrive all-wheel drive. The M4 CS boasts a lighter build compared to the M4 Competition and includes unique design elements and chassis tuning for enhanced performance.
